TextThe Oldstone Alliance is a continent-spanning militia that protects independent towns and villages in Geoctum. Founded by Captain Oldstone after the traumatic event known as the Pull, it promotes self-reliance and mutual aid among mixed-race settlements. The Alliance operates with a decentralized structure led by the Warden-General from Oakhaven, supported by Regional Wardens and local Town Captains. Its primary mission is to defend against banditry and monstrous incursions while fostering a sense of shared identity among diverse communities. The Alliance maintains cautious relationships with other nations, providing stability in areas they cannot control.

The numerous free towns and villages scattered across Geoctum, independent of the major nations, are not left to fend for themselves. They are protected and loosely united by the Oldstone Alliance, a continent-spanning militia faction centralized in the town of Oakhaven. This faction embodies the resilience and self-reliance of those who forged new lives in the "Free Age," a direct legacy of its pragmatic founder.

Origin

The Oldstone Alliance owes its existence directly to the traumatic event of the Pull. When Oakhaven was violently torn from its original world and deposited onto Geoctum, its immediate chaos demanded a leader. Captain Oldstone, then the resolute and pragmatic leader of Oakhaven's small town guard, instinctively rose to the challenge. He rallied his people, and as other small, disoriented communities found themselves similarly adrift, Oldstone, understanding the shared predicament, extended his protection.

The First Alliance

Oldstone, recognizing that scattered villages were easy prey in this strange new world, began forging alliances with nearby "Pulled" or newly established mixed-race settlements. He advocated for mutual support, shared patrols, and a unified response to threats, rather than waiting for help from the larger, insular nations.

Over the centuries, this practical approach spread. As more towns emerged (either through new populations, intermarriage, or the slow expansion of established communities), the idea of a shared defence, organized and managed centrally, took root. The legacy of Captain Oldstone became the guiding principle: self-reliance, mutual aid, and unyielding protection for those without a king or queen.

Structure and Operations

The Oldstone Alliance is a decentralized, yet coordinated, militia, designed for flexibility and rapid response within their local territories, with a strategic command centre in Oakhaven.

  • The Warden-General (Oakhaven-Based): Pipkin Meadowlight, a Halfling whose quiet wisdom and unwavering dedication to the independent towns have solidified the Alliance's role in Geoctum. She embodies the spirit of vigilance and community that Oldstone first championed. She operates from Oakhaven, which functions as the militia's spiritual and strategic headquarters. From here, she coordinates responses to major threats, oversees training standards, and manages resource distribution.
  • Regional Wardens: The vast lands mean the Warden-General relies on Regional Wardens, veteran commanders who oversee a cluster of towns and villages within a specific geographical area (e.g., the Western Plains Warden, the Riverlands Warden).
  • Town Captains & Militias: Each individual mixed-race town or village maintains its own militia, led by a Town Captain (often a local hero or respected elder). These local militias handle day-to-day security and respond to minor threats. When larger dangers emerge, they are reinforced by other local Oldstone Alliance members or through strategic direction from their Regional Warden.

The Alliance is a true reflection of the towns they protect. Their ranks include humans, halflings, half-orcs, gnomes, dwarves, and even individuals from other races who have integrated into these communities. This diversity brings a wide range of skills and perspectives to their defense.

Their primary role is to protect the independent towns and villages from banditry (Ragged Crowns, Grimfang Nomads), monstrous incursions, and the encroaching chaos of larger conflicts. They are the first line of defence for the common person outside the established national borders. By connecting disparate communities, the Oldstone Alliance inadvertently fosters a sense of shared identity among the mixed-race populace, creating a "nation without borders" and subtly encouraging inter-racial cooperation.

Their widespread presence makes them an invaluable source of information about the general state of the free lands, monster movements, and even the subtle activities of other factions outside major cities. As they are not aligned with any one nation, they often serve as neutral parties in disputes between independent towns or even as informal intermediaries in minor border disputes between the major powers.

Relationship with other Nations

Mehdian Republic

They are viewed with cautious respect. The Mehdian Council of Regents recognizes their role in maintaining order in areas they cannot directly control, and occasionally provides limited aid (tools, basic training) in exchange for intelligence. Regent Speaker Bella Cadence would be their staunchest advocate on the Council.

Alfiya

The Alliance often step in to help with disputes between the Elves and the Republic.

The Stoneheart Empire

Generally ignored, who see them as "surface affairs". The Alliance, in turn, focuses on their own territories.

The Wilders

A complex relationship. They often clash over hunting grounds or resource use, but also share a mutual respect for self-reliance. Alliance members might occasionally cooperate with Wilders against a common monstrous threat.
